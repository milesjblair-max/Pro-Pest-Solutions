Who We Are

Pest Management You Can Trust

Pro Pest Solutions delivers professional pest management services to residential homeowners, commercial operators, real estate professionals, and construction companies across the Greater Perth metropolitan area.

We specialise in termite inspections, termite barrier installations, and the treatment of common urban pests — providing clear, detailed reports and practical solutions tailored to each property.

Our service area covers the full Perth metro corridor — from Rockingham in the south through to Alkimos in the north — bringing professional pest management to properties right across the region.
