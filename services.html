Our Services

Pest Management Solutions
for Every Property

Termite inspections, barrier installations, and pest control for residential, commercial, institutional, and construction properties across Greater Perth.

All Services

Termite Inspections

Comprehensive assessment of your property for termite activity, conducive conditions, and structural risk. Inspections are conducted methodically throughout the accessible areas of the building and grounds, with a detailed written report provided upon completion.

Suited to: Homeowners, property buyers, vendors, property managers, and real estate professionals.

Book an Inspection

Termite Barrier Installations

Physical and chemical barrier systems professionally installed to protect properties from termite ingress. We assess the most suitable barrier type for your specific property and install to manufacturer and industry standards.

Suited to: New constructions, existing residential homes, commercial buildings, and investment properties.

General Pest Treatments

Targeted treatment of common urban pests including cockroaches, ants, spiders, silverfish, wasps, and other pest species. Treatments are applied safely and effectively with minimal disruption to the property and its occupants.

Suited to: Residential homes, apartment buildings, commercial premises, and institutional facilities.

Book a Treatment

Residential Pest Management

Tailored pest management programs for homes, units, and residential properties. We address current infestations, identify entry points and conducive conditions, and provide practical recommendations to reduce ongoing pest pressure.

Suited to: Homeowners, tenants, landlords, and property managers across the Perth metro area.

Book a Service

Commercial Pest Management

Discreet, effective, and compliant pest management for commercial environments. We work around your operating hours and business requirements to deliver thorough pest control with minimum disruption to your operations.

Suited to: Retail premises, offices, hospitality venues, warehouses, industrial facilities, and managed commercial buildings.

Real Estate Pest Services

Pre-sale and pre-purchase termite inspections, timber pest reports, and general pest assessments to support property transactions. Fast turnaround on reports to keep settlements on track.

Suited to: Real estate agents, property managers, vendors preparing for sale, and buyers conducting due diligence.

Book an Inspection

Construction Pest Management

Pre-construction termite management is a critical component of the building process in Perth's termite-active environment. We provide chemical and physical pre-construction termite protection systems installed to meet relevant Australian Standards, along with compliance documentation required by councils and certifiers.

We also provide on-going pest management support throughout the construction phase — including site inspections, monitoring, and treatment as required to protect your build from termite risk.

Suited to: Builders, developers, project managers, and construction companies operating across the Greater Perth metro area.
